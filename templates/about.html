About Us

Founded in 1993, our jewelry shop began as a humble endeavor fueled by passion and dedication. With a small amount of gold and the unwavering support of my brothers, we started crafting beautiful pieces that symbolized love, tradition, and celebration. Over the years, our commitment to quality and customer trust has helped us grow beyond expectations. Today, we proudly serve our customers from multiple locations in Devara Hipparagi, offering a wide range of exquisite jewelry for every occasion. At Praveen Jewellers, every piece tells a story of fine craftsmanship, timeless elegance, and unmatched precision. We are honored to be a part of your special moments, creating treasures that last a lifetime.
