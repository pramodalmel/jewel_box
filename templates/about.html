Our Story

Our Heritage

Founded in 1993, Praveen Jewellers began as a humble endeavor fueled by passion and dedication. With a small amount of gold and the unwavering support of our family, we started crafting beautiful pieces that symbolized love, tradition, and celebration.

Over the years, our commitment to quality and customer trust has helped us grow beyond expectations. Today, we proudly serve our customers from multiple locations in Devara Hipparagi.
